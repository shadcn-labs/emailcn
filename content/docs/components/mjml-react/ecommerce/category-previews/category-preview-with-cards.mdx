| `variant` | `"basic" \| "with-header" \| "with-description" \| "header-description" \| "with-details" \| "header-details" \| "full-details" \| "header-full-details"` | `"with-header"` | Mailviews content-density variant |
| `theme`   | `EmailThemeTokens`                                                                                                                                        | `defaultTheme`  | Email theme styling               |
